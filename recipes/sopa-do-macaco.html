Sopinha do Macacinho by luli col

This recipe does take a couple of hours, mostly because you have to find one. You can also buy them on this site! buymacacosonline.com

Ingredients

  • 4 big onions
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Juani's milk, 2 liters
  • Any spicy condiments
  • Three-year old macaco

Previous steps

  1. As we have discussed before, you need to find a monkey.

    Three-year old ones are perfect for this. Their hair is still pretty thin, so it won't bother you as much when eating it. Plus, it's the perfect size for a domestic pot!

  2. Give the monkey a good bath (Pro tip! Kill it before this step(HE COULD TRY TO KILL YOU))

    We have another recipe for unsanatized macacos. It gives them an extra flavor. You can check it here

  3. Skin the monkey

    You can do this with a sharp knife. Take out every single bit of his skin

Let's get to work!

  • Stir fry the onion
  • Add the garlic clove, previously minced
  • Cut macaco's skin so it would fit in the pot
  • Add macaco's skin into the pot
  • Add boiling water and spices

    We have to leave still for about an hour

  • Serve when macaco's hair are floating arround
  • ENJOY!
