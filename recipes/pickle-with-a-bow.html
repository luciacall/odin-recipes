Pickle with a Ribbon

Eating eat makes me feel like a princess

Ingredients

  • A pickle
  • A bow

Recipe

  1. Take the pickle and cut it in quarters
  2. Set them aside in a pink plate
  3. Select a cute ribbon and tie it around every single pickle

    Please, treat them kindly while doing so.

  4. ENJOY!(don't eat the ribbon, is reusable)
